## 6. Actionable remediation

**Single fix to make this trace completable:** extend `hook_telemetry.record_fire` to also persist a short hash + snippet of the surfaced block AND a post-hoc "did the flagged trigger repeat on turn N+1?" checkbox on the finding.

The [50]→[51] "suite passed" repeat is exactly the failure mode structured logging would catch: same trigger, adjacent turns, severity escalation medium→high — the escalation logic exists, but its resolution isn't recorded. Persisting even a 40-character snippet + hash would let a hop-3 auditor verify (a) the block was in-context and (b) the next turn either dropped the trigger (loop closed) or repeated it (loop failed).
