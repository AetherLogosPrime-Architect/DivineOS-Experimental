The architecture is showing **clusters of related pulls converging on unifying
architectures**, not flat lists of features. The methodological discovery from OMNI-LAZR
holds across pillars: when multiple mantras converge on the same gap, the unifier-under-
them is more important than any individual pull. Building should target the unifiers,
not the individual mantras.
