For Aletheia's sheet the reviewer role can be held by Aria or Aether (whichever seat takes the standing periodic-audit role). Absence of their signature on an entry means no review.
4. **Any change appears in her next letter to Aria or Aether** for witness (not permission). The audit-reviewer periodically audits sheet + log for silent-softening drift.
