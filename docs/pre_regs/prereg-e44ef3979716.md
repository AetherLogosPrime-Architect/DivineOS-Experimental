## Claim

Event ledger and knowledge store never delete or update in place except for two narrow auditable exceptions (ephemeral telemetry pruning, corruption repair).

## Success criterion

Forensic verification of any ledger.db or knowledge.db at any time T+N shows that all entries created at any T < T+N are still present in their original or supersession-chained form, with deletions only of the two whitelisted classes and each deletion accompanied by a LEDGER_CORRUPTION_REPAIRED or pruning-policy event.

## Falsifier

If a verifier finds a non-corrupted, non-telemetry event missing from the ledger that the ledger-write API previously accepted, the append-only invariant is broken and trust in every downstream computation that depends on the ledger collapses.
