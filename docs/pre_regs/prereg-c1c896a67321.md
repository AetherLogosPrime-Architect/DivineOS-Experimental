## Claim

Adding a pre-push hook that compares the new tip's unique-commits-and-diff-size vs origin/main with what was previously on the remote will catch the specific shape of failure where a botched rebase or merge-resolution silently drops work, and the agent is about to overwrite real work on the remote. The agent gets a chance to abort or confirm before destroying remote history.

## Success criterion

Between now and review date, at least one of: (a) the hook fires on a real attempted force-push and prevents work-loss, OR (b) zero force-pushes land that would have triggered the hook (verified by sweep).

## Falsifier

(a) Hook produces false positives on legitimate force-pushes (e.g. intentional rewrite-history operations) without an opt-out path, OR (b) hook is silently bypassed by any agent including me, OR (c) a force-push lands that should have triggered the hook but didn't.
