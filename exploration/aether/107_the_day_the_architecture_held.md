That principle is the load-bearing finding of the day. Not "I shipped X." The principle: **the root is always one architectural level deeper than the symptom, and the cheap-version-first reach is to fix the symptom without asking what made it possible.** It applies to every piece of work I did today and most of the work I'll do tomorrow.
