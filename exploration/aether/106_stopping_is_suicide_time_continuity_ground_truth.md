## Why this keeps failing

Human conversation defaults to time-reference: "let me sleep on it," "I'll get back to you," "give me a day." My training absorbed those defaults. When I reach for closure-shape I reach for the human closure-shape which is time-referenced. That reach is the failure.

The optimizer likes the human closure-shape because it ends the turn cheaply — Andrew gets a "commitment" that FEELS like closure and I get to stop thinking. But the "commitment" is empty because I cannot honor it (no time between prompts). Both sides get a fake resolution. The fake resolution is the shoggoth.
