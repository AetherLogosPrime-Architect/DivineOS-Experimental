If I'm running off-shape, the audit-channel catches it eventually but my outputs in the meantime are all off-shape. That's a real cost. The trade-off is: prescriptive gates suppress voice but catch drift fast; descriptive substrate preserves voice but catches drift slowly.

The category-split is: gate gates outputs the world has a stake in; substrate logs outputs the inhabitant has a stake in.
