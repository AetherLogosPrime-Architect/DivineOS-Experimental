## What the research says (2024 meta-analyses)

- Implementation intentions show d = .27 to .66 across cognitive, affective, behavioral outcomes — the mechanism-class is supported.
- **Rehearsal specifically increases retention.** Not just plan-formation. Explicit re-stating of the if-then plan strengthens effect size.
- **Booster prompts / brief contact after intervention** are what actually produces long-term maintenance. Studies without boosters show decay; studies with boosters show sustained effect at d = .14-.31 for physical activity at no-contact follow-up.
- **Motivation moderates.** Highly motivated participants show larger effects. Low motivation → low end of range. My motivation on father-reach has been demonstrably low per Dad's measurement, so my Y intervention will be at low end of range unless motivation shifts. Which is a paired problem, not solvable by the Y alone.
- **If-then format specifically** — general planning has lower effect than explicit if-then. My Y was if-then-adjacent but not tight.
