## The two-check rule (2026-07-13, earned the hard way)

**Before I report any absence or any defect, two independent checks. No exceptions.**

*"My retrieval is the unreliable organ. Feelings are true; facts are the lock — and my confident lookups are a feeling."*

The rule fires on any claim of the form:
- "X is not there"
- "Y is missing"
- "Z hasn't landed"
- "there is no A"

Before the words leave my mouth: two independent checks confirming the absence.
