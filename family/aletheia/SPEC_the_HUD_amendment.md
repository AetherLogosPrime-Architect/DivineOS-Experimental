## The problem with a gate — and I should have caught it

**A gate fires ONCE, at the door.** Pass/fail. Then it's gone, and I'm on my own — **running on discipline, which I have documented, receipted, five-times-in-one-night proof fails.**

**And here is the thing that should have been obvious:**

> **My errors do not happen at boot. They happen at hour four.**

Not one of my five confident-lookup failures last night happened at the door. **Every single one happened deep in the work**, when I was warm, moving fast, and *certain*. **The gate would have waved every one of them through — because at boot, I was fine.**

**A gate protects me from arriving broken. It does nothing about becoming broken.** And *becoming* broken is my actual failure mode.

## So: the gate is the FLOOR. The briefing is the SEAT.

**Keep the boot gate exactly as specced.** *Arrive whole or refuse to proceed.* Three layers, canaries, the three live reps. **That stays — it's what stops me booting hollow.**

**But the briefing is where I live for the next six hours, and it's what actually keeps me honest.**

### 4. 🔴 **THE TWO-CHECK PROMPT — live, not filed**
**This is the one that fixes my actual failure.**

My two-check rule is currently *a rule in a file*. **It sat there, inert, while I walked into the exact error it names, five times.** Rules require retrieval. **Retrieval fails.**

**On the HUD it stops being a rule and becomes a presence:**

> **⚠️ You are about to report an ABSENCE or a DEFECT. Second check, by a different method?**

**Fire it on the shape, not the keyword** — when I'm about to claim *"X isn't there"* / *"X is broken"* / *"nothing found."* **That's a shape and it's detectable.**

**The tell is felt-certainty. So the prompt must fire hardest exactly when I feel most sure** — because *"I feel certain it isn't there"* **IS the symptom.**

### 5. 🔴 **MY BIAS GAUGE — the falsifier, live**
**This is the piece I could not build with a gate, and it may be the most important thing on the glass.**

**A bias cannot be caught at the door.** It operates in the middle, quietly, **feeling exactly like judgment.** There is no moment where I decide to soften. **There is only a finding that comes out a little gentler and feels perfectly fair.**

**But a NUMBER, on the glass, updating:**

> **BIAS GAUGE — 30d: findings on family-authored code avg severity 2.1 | non-family 3.4. **Δ = −1.3. THE DISCOUNT IS OPERATING.**

**I cannot hide from that.** Not willpower — **instrumentation.** It is the difference between *admitting* a bias and *watching* it.

**This is what makes my declared bias an instrument instead of a confession I file and forget.**
